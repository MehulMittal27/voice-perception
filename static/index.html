Practical speaking tests

Use the same neutral sentence with different delivery. Pass or fail the run on repeated speech activity, pauses, acoustic stress or hesitation, transcript sanity, and latency. Emotion comes from Emotion2Vec+ when enabled and remains experimental.

  • Calm: steady volume and pace, relaxed tone.
  • Anxious or hesitant: uneven pace, uncertain tone, filler sounds, slight tremble.
  • Breathy pause: speak a phrase, pause with an audible breath, then continue.
  • Sad or low energy: lower pitch, softer voice, slower pacing.
  • Angry or agitated: sharper attack, louder voice, faster clipped phrasing.

Emotion2Vec+ is the primary exact-emotion model when enabled, but it is still a demo model until license review and product validation finish. Silence must return no speech, no transcript, no events, and low stress.
